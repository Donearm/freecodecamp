Features

  • Premium Ingredients

    We only use original ingredients from Italy, Caputo flour, San Marzano tomatoes, buffalo mozzarella from Campania and sea salt. No lower-quality ingredients would create the genuine experience that we strive to offer.

  • Fast Shipping

    A cold pizza won't do justice to its quality. That's why we have teamed with a squad of fast couriers that will deliver you the pizza as hot as from the oven.

  • Made with Love

    There are those who make pizza for a living. There are those who make pizza for money. We make it for the love of it. All our pizzamen and pizzawomen have an authentic love for the art of making pizza and we won't accept anything less than passion. Love is what makes our pizza special, the ultimate ingredient we like to call it.
